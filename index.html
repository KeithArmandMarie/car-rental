FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I make a reservation?

Making a reservation is easy! Simply visit our ‘Car Showroom’ page, choose the car you desire, select your rental dates, and click ‘Book Now.’ Follow the straightforward prompts to complete your reservation.

What documents are required for renting a car?

To rent a car, you need a valid driver’s license, proof of identity, and a credit/debit card for payment. Some vehicles may have additional requirements, so be sure to check the car’s listing for any specific conditions.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can! We understand plans change. Log in to your account, go to ‘Reservation Management,’ and you’ll find options to modify or cancel your booking. Please review our cancellation policy for any associated fees.

What's included in the rental rate?

Our rental rates typically cover the cost of the car, standard insurance, and any additional amenities specified in the listing. Any extra charges, such as fuel or optional add-ons, will be detailed during the booking process.

How do I return the car after my rental period ends?

Returning the car is hassle-free. You’ll return it to the same location where you picked it up, unless stated otherwise in the rental agreement. Ensure the car is in the same condition and with the same fuel level as when you received it.
